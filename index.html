Are Precious Metals Right For You?

A strong defense against dedollarization and inflation is offered by precious metals. Think about it this way: gold and silver are safely kept in a safe or depository, staying within your grasp, in case other investment or savings routes fail. Silver and gold, in contrast to money, cannot be produced by the state. They act as a type of safety net against turbulence in the economy or political/military unrest. Precious metals are separate from the digital financial world in situations where traditional investing options are difficult or fail. Their safety from turmoil makes them the best asset for a secure haven.
Gold
Unlike the US dollar, which has lost 98% of its purchasing power since 1971, gold’s purchasing power remains stable over time. It has proven its worth in the face of inflation, recession, currency fluctuation, market collapse and social upheaval.
